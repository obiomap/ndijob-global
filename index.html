Who We Are

One Bond. One Family.
One Obowo Land.

We are the Asonye Realest Group — NdiJob Global — a family bound not by paperwork or formality, but by something far deeper: the childhood bonds forged growing up together across Obowo land. Formally founded on April 26, 2025, what we became had already been years in the making.

We ran the same dusty paths, sat under the same trees, shared the same laughter and the same hardships. Those early years planted something in us that no amount of distance or time has been able to uproot. That is what unites us — not just where we are from, but who we became together.

Our family includes members from communities within Obowo, and proudly extends to our sisters who are married both within and outside Obowo. Wherever life has taken them, the childhood bond holds. They are as much a part of NdiJob Global as anyone — same circle, same love, same roots.

What defines us is simple: Love, Respect, and the joy of catching fun together — for one another, for our families, and for the communities across Obowo that shaped us from the very beginning. Our motto says it all: United in Love.

Our first homecoming in December 2025 brought us back together on home soil — a day of joy, reunion, and celebration that reminded us exactly why this family matters. We are already looking forward to the second homecoming on December 27, 2026, and everything it will mean for our people and our communities.
